# Gravity Flip

## Overview
Gravity Flip is a 2D physics-based platformer developed as an Applied Physics course project. The game allows players to reverse gravity to navigate obstacles, avoid hazards, and reach the finish flag.

---

## Game Description
Players control a character that can move left, move right, jump, and flip gravity. By changing the direction of gravity, the player can move between the floor and ceiling to solve platforming challenges.

---

## Features
- Gravity inversion mechanic
- Physics-based movement
- Jumping system
- Hazards and obstacles
- Win condition and level completion
- Lightweight and optimized design

---

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| A | Move Left |
| D | Move Right |
| Space | Jump |
| G | Flip Gravity |

---

## Physics Concepts Used

### Gravity
The game demonstrates gravitational acceleration by pulling the player toward the floor or ceiling depending on the current gravity state.

### Acceleration
The player's velocity changes continuously due to gravity and jumping forces.

### Velocity
Player movement and jumping are based on velocity changes over time.

### Force
Jumping applies an upward force while gravity applies a downward force.

### Collision Detection
The player interacts with platforms, hazards, and the finish flag using collision detection.

### Newton's Laws of Motion
The player's movement follows the principles of force, acceleration, and inertia.
